## üí° About the 42 Discovery Piscine

The **Discovery Piscine** is an introductory program offered by **42 Porto**. It serves as a "first dive" into the world of coding and the unique 42 methodology. Over a shorter period than the main Piscine, participants explore the fundamentals of web development and logical thinking.

* **Peer-to-Peer Learning:** Even in the Discovery phase, the focus is on collaborating with peers and finding solutions without traditional teachers.
* **Tech Stack:** Primarily focused on **HTML**, **CSS**, and **JavaScript**, providing the tools to build first web applications.
